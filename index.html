Ketuv — English and Greek New Testament Study

See Scripture side‑by‑side in English and the original Greek.

About Ketuv

Ketuv was born out of a passion to see Scripture the way it was written, in its original language, and to help others do the same. After reading the Bible and diving into Koine Greek, I realized how much depth can be lost in translation. Ketuv aims to bridge that gap by offering parallel text, audio, and tools for learning while you study.

The app will offer:

  • Parallel English & Greek verses
  • Verse‑by‑verse audio
  • Greek vocabulary and morphology lookup
  • Cross references and commentary
  • Study tools for learners of Greek

Why Learn Greek Through Scripture?

While translations faithfully communicate the meaning of Scripture, nuances in the original Greek can bring fresh insight into word choices, grammar, and emphasis that shape how we understand God’s Word.

By studying the Greek alongside the English, you’ll gain:

  • Deeper insight into theological terms
  • Stronger understanding of the New Testament writers
  • Tools for personal growth and ministry

Features

Parallel Text

English New Testament with original Greek beneath each verse.

Interactive Greek Lookup

Tap any Greek word to see definitions, parsing, and lexical info.

Audio Playback

Listen to verse‑by‑verse narration in both languages.

Study Tools

Integrated notes, learned word lists, quizzes, and contextual commentary.

How It Works

  1. Select a book and chapter.
  2. View the English verse on top and Greek beneath.
  3. Tap any Greek word to see definitions & parsing.
  4. Use learn mode to study vocabulary and grammar.
  5. Save notes and build your own library.

Built With

  • React / Next.js (Website)
  • Android App with Kotlin + Jetpack Compose
  • SQLite for Scripture storage
  • Open or Public Domain Greek/English Texts
  • Optional LLM for intelligent word help
